FAQ

What is GamerzParadise?

GamerzParadise is a digital distribution platform with a curated selection of games.

Which platform GamerzParadise Support?

Currently GamerzParadise is supported for PC only.

Are there any charges to access GamerzParadise?

No. GamerzParaide is free to access but you have to pay to buy a game on respective marchents sites.

How can I purchase a game?

Click on Shop Now or head towards Buy Now section then choose your favourite marchant and place your order.

How can I know if a game is compatable to my system?

You can always check in system requirements section to make sure that minimum requirements match with your system for a specific game.

Will I get game in a CD or as a soft copy?

It depends upon from which marchant you buy a particular game. e.g. if you buy game from steam you will get a soft copy whose license will be with your steam id. Whereas if you buy from amazon you may get a physical disk.

What if I can't find a particular game on site?

If a particular game is not present on our site then please contact to one of our developer so that we can add it.
